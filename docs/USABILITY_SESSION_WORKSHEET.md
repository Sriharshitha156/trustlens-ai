# TrustLens AI — Usability Session Worksheet (Ready to Fill)

> This is the "just sit a person down and fill the blanks" version of the test plan.
> Run it **5 times** (one person at a time, ~20 min each). One filled sheet per person.
> When all 5 are done, copy the numbers into `USABILITY_TEST_REPORT.md`.

---

## ⭐ FACILITATOR QUICK CARD (read this once before you start)

**Setup each time:** open the app, set role to **IT Administrator**, go to **Dashboard**.
Start screen recording (**Win + Alt + R**).

**Say this to begin (read aloud):**
> "Thanks for helping! I'm testing the *interface*, not you — there are no wrong answers.
> Please **think out loud**: say what you see, what you expect, and anything that confuses you.
> I'll record the screen and your voice just for our notes. Ready?"

**Golden rules while they work:**
- Give **one task at a time**. Don't explain the screen.
- If they go quiet, say: *"What are you thinking right now?"*
- If stuck >60 seconds, say *"that's useful, let's move on"* and mark the task **Fail**.
- Don't point at the screen or hint. Their confusion IS your result.
